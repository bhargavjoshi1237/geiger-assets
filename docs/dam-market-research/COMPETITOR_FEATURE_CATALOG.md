# Digital Asset Management Competitor Feature Catalog

Research date: June 14, 2026

This document consolidates the publicly documented capabilities of the DAM products and adjacent platforms reviewed for Geiger Assets.

## Evidence Legend

- **Confirmed:** Explicitly described in the reviewed public product material.
- **Module:** Available through a named module, add-on, enterprise package, or closely integrated product.
- **Not confirmed:** Not found in the reviewed public material. This does not prove the product lacks the capability.

The machine-readable normalized comparison remains in [feature_matrix.csv](feature_matrix.csv). Detailed links are in [sources.md](sources.md).
