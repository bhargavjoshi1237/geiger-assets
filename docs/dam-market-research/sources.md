# DAM Market Research Sources

Research date: June 14, 2026

## Method

- The core comparison set contains 12 products selected to cover enterprise DAM suites, modern collaborative DAMs, developer-first media platforms, and visual media teams.
- `C` in the feature matrix means the capability is explicitly supported in current public product material.
- `M` means the capability is available through a named module, add-on, adjacent product, enterprise tier, or closely integrated product surface.
- `U` means the capability was not confirmed in the public material reviewed. It does not prove the product lacks the capability.
- Prevalence counts treat `C` and `M` as supported.
- Pricing is recorded only when the vendor publishes a usable price. Quote-only vendors are not assigned estimated prices.

## Important Evidence Limits

- Public marketing pages are not implementation documentation. Some enterprise capabilities may exist but remain unconfirmed because they are sold through solution consulting.
- Vendor bundles and prices can change. Pricing should be rechecked before any launch-price decision.
- Gallery commerce was evaluated as a buyer-facing storefront with checkout, not merely a branded portal or public share page.
- Outbound license management was evaluated as a record of rights granted to a buyer or licensee, including scope, term, payment, and revenue. Basic asset expiration metadata does not satisfy this definition.
